3 Entities

3.1 Text Retrieval Conference (TREC) Video Retrieval Evaluation: TRECVID

TRECVID data sets are available here: https://trecvid.nist.gov/trecvid.data.html

3.2 The Linguistic Data Consortium (LDC)

LDC engaged in a project called Heterogeneous Audio Visual Internet Collection (HAVIC) as part of TRECVID 2012.

LDC also has a Video Annotation for Speech Technologies (VAST) project. Here is a paper describing the project. A VScout browser interface for viewing and annotating web video was developed for this project. VAST contains 2,900 hours of video.

3.2.1 Licensing

LDC data licensing terms are here: https://www.ldc.upenn.edu/data-management/using/licensing. These are based on membership agreements that are distinct for not-for-profit, for-profit, and government entities.

Not-for-profit memberships are $2,400/year. Subscription memberships (automatically receive two copies on media of all datasets released in the membership year) are $3,850. For-profit memberships are $24,000 and $27,500/year. Government entities are charged 2,400/year.

There is also an agreement for non-members. It appears that many of the datasets are governed by corpus-specific licenses. Here is an example of a non-member user license agreement.

3.2.2 Administration
